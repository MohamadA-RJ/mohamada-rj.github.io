## PhD Announcement & Research Focus

I'm excited to share that I have started my PhD at the <a href="https://www.tudelft.nl/en/ae/organisation/departments/aerospace-structures-and-materials" style="color: #00A6D6; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">Aerospace Structures and Materials (ASM)</a> department, part of the <a href="https://www.tudelft.nl/en/ae" style="color: #00A6D6; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">Faculty of Aerospace Engineering</a> at TU Delft. This is within the <a href="https://luchtvaartintransitie.nl/en/" style="color: #00A6D6; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">'Luchtvaart in Transitie' (Aviation in Transition)</a> initiative.

My research focuses on advancing lightweight composites and structures using computational mechanics and deep learning technologies, under the supervision of:

- <strong>Dr. Boyang Chen:</strong> <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/boyangchenswebsite/welcome" target="_blank">Boyang Chen's Website</a>
- <strong>Dr. Clemens Dransfeld:</strong> <a href="https://www.paam-lab.org/" target="_blank">PAAM Lab Website</a>
